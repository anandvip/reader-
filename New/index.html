📖 How to Use Enhanced TTS Reader

🚀 Getting Started

1. Add Content: Paste or type text into the main area

2. Customize: Click the gear icon ⚙️ to adjust voice, speed, and volume

3. Listen: Hit "Read Aloud" and let AI chapters guide your learning

💾 Knowledge Management

Save Important Content: Click the bookmark icon 🔖 to add to your library

Organize Learning: Build a collection of articles, papers, and insights

Quick Access: Resume reading from any chapter, anytime

🎯 Smart Features

Auto-Chapters: Long content automatically splits into manageable sections

Smart Resume: Switch apps and seamlessly resume where you left off

Progress Tracking: Visual indicators show your reading progress

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts
Ctrl + Enter Start/Stop Reading
Space Quick Play/Pause
Escape Stop Reading / Close Modal

📱 Mobile Experience

Touch-Optimized: All controls work perfectly on phones and tablets

Screen Lock: Prevents screen from turning off during reading

Background Playback: Continues reading when you switch apps
